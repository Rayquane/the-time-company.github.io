Questions

Why was this website created ?

It's an idea that came from fun discussions between the two of us. As Nefarious Tropy fans, we saw the community absolutely not respecting them in general. I, as Rayquane, thought of a kind of a tribute website. Something to talk about their legacy, who they are, factually, while respecting them.
We think the community and the most recent developers, as of the end of 2024 (Toys for Bob who did IAT and Rumble), didn't give them justice and support.

Why are the terms Male Tropy, Female Tropy and Prime Tropy not used ?

We find the two first terms to be degrading and not respectful about the interpretations of the character and prefer to not use them, as it's only refering to them by their gender. They are two different interpretations, not just two N. Tropy in two different genders.
The term of Prime Tropy, as much as it is factual that Sir Tropy existed before Lady Tropy, makes it feels like Lady Tropy is inferior to Sir Tropy.

What are the terms used then ?

To compare them :
- Sir Tropy and Lady Tropy
- N. Tropy and N. Tropy (Alt)

Otherwise, we simply use Doctor Nefarious Tropy for both of them.

Why is N. Tropy from Crash Bandicoot 4 : It's About Time not presented in the site at all ?

We'll keep it simple and honest : this is not N. Tropy.

Can I contribute ?

As we lost faith in the community... No.
